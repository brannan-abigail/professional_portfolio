Howdy!

I'm Abigail Brannan, an aspiring Software Engineer studying Computer Science Major at Texas A&M. I am interested in this field because I enjoy solving problems, which feels like solving a puzzle, while getting to continuously learn new languages, philosophies and techniques, and making life easier for people. Technology is a powerful tool for making positive change, and it's exciting to be a part of it!

Technology has a hardware component as well as software, which I work with at the Fischer Engineering Design Center, the Engineering department's large makerspace. Working there is always interesting as there are many different machines and methods of manufacturing to learn. So far, I've learned the Desktop 3D Printers, and specialize in the PCB machines. There's still so much more to learn!

Outside of work and school, I enjoy relaxing at home trying new recipes, attempting to keep plants alive, doing yoga and reading books.
